About Next Gen Transport L.L.C

Two decades of dependable logistics and transport excellence

Next Gen Transport L.L.C is a strategic joint venture between Urban Cool Transport L.L.C and Western Link Equipment Rental L.L.C — two of the UAE's most established logistics and transport companies with a combined legacy of over 18 years of operational excellence in cold chain and general cargo transportation.
