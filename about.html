About Us

Driving the future of logistics with innovation and excellence

Next Gen Transport

Next Gen Transport L.L.C is a strategic joint venture between Urban Cool Transport L.L.C and Western Link Equipment Rental L.L.C, two of the UAE's most established logistics and transport companies with a combined legacy of over 18 years of operational excellence in cold chain and general cargo transportation.

This partnership was formed to unify expertise, technology, and resources under one powerful identity — creating a smarter, stronger, and more sustainable logistics brand for the future. Operating under the unified name Next Gen Transport L.L.C, the company delivers enhanced capacity, innovation, and reliability across the UAE and GCC.

From temperature-controlled reefer transport to heavy-duty industrial logistics, Next Gen Transport provides precision-driven, compliant, and technology-enabled solutions that ensure every shipment is handled with care, visibility, and efficiency. We go beyond transport — we deliver trust, transparency, and technological excellence.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to redefine logistics in the region by creating a smart, sustainable, and technology-led transport ecosystem that delivers efficiency, precision, and value to every client, every time. We aim to set a new benchmark in the logistics industry through innovation, reliability, and service excellence, ensuring every movement contributes to a more connected and sustainable future.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to lead the next generation of logistics through digital tracking, eco-efficient operations, and temperature-controlled expertise — delivering seamless, dependable, and compliant transport solutions that empower our partners to grow confidently. We are committed to advancing logistics standards through smart technology, operational precision, and customer-focused excellence.

OUR CORE VALUES

At Next Gen Transport, our core values define who we are and guide how we operate every day:

Innovation

We drive logistics forward through data, automation, and advanced fleet technology, constantly seeking smarter ways to enhance efficiency and reliability.

Sustainability

We are committed to eco-conscious practices that minimize our environmental footprint, ensuring that growth and responsibility go hand in hand.

Together, these values form the foundation of our operations, helping us deliver excellence while shaping a sustainable future for the logistics industry.
